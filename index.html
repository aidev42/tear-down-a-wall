Mission

Tear Down a Wall is a campaign for a more open world inspired by the fall of one of the world's most iconic walls- the Berlin Wall- on November 9th, 1989.

We believe a worldwide day dedicated to the opening of arms and minds has a tangible impact in making the world a better place, and that big change is the product of lots of people making little changes to their daily lives.

Each Nov 9th, Tear Down a Wall Day is a chance to reflect on the barriers in your life- mental, physical, or otherwise- and share your story about knocking them down.
