#TearDownAWall

No politics. No agendas. Just humans for a more open world.

Mission

Tear Down a Wall is a campaign for a more open world inspired by the fall of one of the world's most iconic walls- the Berlin Wall- on November 9th, 1989.

We believe dedicating a day to the opening of arms and minds has a tangible impact in making the world a better place, and that big change is the product of lots of people making little changes to their daily lives.

Tear Down a Wall Day is a chance to reflect on the barriers in your life- mental, physical, or otherwise- and make a real change by tearing one down. On Nov 9th, should you make a difference, or will you?
